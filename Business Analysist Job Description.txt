Job Title: Lead Business Analyst
Role Overview
As a Lead Business Analyst, you will take on an advanced leadership role, managing multiple teams of analysts while driving client engagement and solution innovation. You will work closely with onshore Solutions Leads, Senior Consultants, and Partners, serving as the bridge between client business challenges and DT Consulting’s cutting-edge solution suite. This role involves strategic problem-solving, data-driven insights, and hands-on team leadership to ensure high-quality consulting deliverables.
Key Responsibilities
Team & Project Leadership
•    Lead and mentor teams of Senior Business Analysts and Business Analysts (5-10 team members).
•    Define project scopes, objectives, and deliverables, ensuring alignment with client goals and expectations.
•    Oversee workflow management, resource allocation, and execution of multiple consulting engagements.
•    Serve as the final quality checkpoint for analytical outputs, ensuring consistency, accuracy, and strategic relevance.
•    Develop and enforce best practices for data collection, research methodologies, and issue resolution.
Client & Stakeholder Engagement
•    Lead client discussions and presentations, offering deep insights and data-backed recommendations.
•    Act as the primary offshore liaison for onshore solution leads and senior consulting teams.
•    Provide strategic direction for digital transformation and customer experience initiatives.
•    Strengthen client relationships, identifying opportunities for expanding service offerings.
Data Analysis & Solution Development
•    Oversee complex data manipulation, quantitative research, and qualitative analysis.
•    Lead the design, execution, and interpretation of advanced analytics, dashboards, and reporting frameworks.
•    Partner with solution development teams to enhance existing methodologies and create new data-driven insights.
•    Utilize Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Python, and advanced Excel for high-impact data analysis.
•    Develop and optimize processes for efficiency, risk management, and operational excellence.
Qualifications & Experience
•    7+ years of experience in business analysis, consulting, data analytics, or digital transformation.
•    Proven track record in leading teams, managing projects, and delivering client-facing consulting solutions.
•    Expertise in data-driven decision-making, advanced Excel modeling, SQL, Python, and business intelligence tools.
•    Experience in CX, UX, and digital transformation strategies within the life sciences industry (preferred).
•    Strong ability to translate complex datasets into actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.
•    Master’s or Bachelor’s degree in Business, Data Science, Analytics, or a related field.
Why Join DT Consulting?
